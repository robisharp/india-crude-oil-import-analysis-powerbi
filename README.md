# india-crude-oil-import-analysis-powerbi
Power BI dashboard analyzing India’s crude oil imports (2018–2023) with insights on pricing, country trends, and post-2020 geopolitical shifts including Russia’s discounted oil impact.

👉 This project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to understand global energy trends and geopolitical impact on India’s oil imports.

📝 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes India’s crude oil imports from 2018 to 2023, focusing on:

Import quantity and value

Price trends over time

Country-wise contribution

Forecast insights with real-world interpretation

🔍 Key Insights

🌍 Major Import Partners

Iraq remained the largest supplier

Saudi Arabia held a consistent second position

UAE and USA contributed moderately

Russia became significant after 2020

📉 Post-2020 Market Shift

Russia entered strongly into India’s import mix after 2020

This shift was driven by global geopolitical changes

India diversified sourcing strategies to optimize cost and supply

💰 Pricing Dynamics

Middle East countries maintained stable pricing

Russian crude oil was offered at discounted prices

India leveraged this price advantage to reduce import costs

📈 Trend Analysis

Decline in 2020 due to global disruptions

Strong recovery from 2021 onwards

Rising demand reflects India’s economic growth

⚠️ Forecast Insight 

Forecast indicates a rising trend in imports

However, geopolitical tensions (wars, sanctions, supply chain disruptions) may impact:

Price volatility

Supplier dependency

Future import strategies
